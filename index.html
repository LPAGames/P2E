Publishing your Games

Understanding the Prize Pool:

When publishing games with us you choose the price for sell and from 10% -> 35% for the prize pool.

From:

15% - For our platform
10% - for Pool
75% - for you

Until:

15% - for our platform
35% - for Pool
50% - for you

The Pool will be used for prizes in your games.
The player will be reward when achivie something determined in your game.
